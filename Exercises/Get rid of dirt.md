You have a list
```house = ['dirt','furniture','tv','plant','cup','furniture','lamp','dirt','drapes','sofa','computer','dirt']```

You want to remove all ```'dirt'``` in the ```house```

<details>
  <summary>answer</summary>
  
  ```
  while "dirt" in house:
      house.remove("dirt")
  print(house)
  ```
</details>
